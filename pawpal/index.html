About

A pet sitter based right here in Pune

I started PawPal because I know what it's like to worry about leaving a pet behind — with a kennel that feels cold, or a sitter who's really just watching the clock. Every pet I look after gets treated the way I'd want mine treated: fed on time, walked properly, and never left alone longer than they're used to.

  • Comfortable with dogs, cats, and small pets
  • Comfortable giving routine medication
  • Available for both short visits and multi-day stays
